About Blossom

Blossom Bogen-Froese is an artist specializing in mixed-media installations and contemporary sculptures. With a passion for exploring textures and materials, she creates immersive works that invite viewers to engage with the art on a personal level.

Featured Work: "Untitled Sculpture"

This sculpture was inspired by the interplay of light and shadow. It’s made from recycled materials and explores themes of transformation and renewal.
